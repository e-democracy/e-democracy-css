Invite a New Group Member

To invite a new member to the group, enter his or her details below and click the Invite button. A profile will be created, and an email will be sent inviting the recipient to join Minneapolis Northeast Neighbors Forum. You must fill out all required fields, which are marked .

Invitation

An invitation is an email from you asking the recipient to join Minneapolis Northeast Neighbors Forum. You can customise the message below.

Email From (required)

The email address that you want in the "From" line in the invitation that you send.

Email To (required)

The email address of the new group member.The invitation will be sent to this address, and the address will become the default address for the new group member.

Subject (required)

The subject line of the invitation message that will be sent to the new member

Invitation Message (required)

The message that appears at the top of the email invitation to the new group member. The message will appear before the link that allows the member to accept or reject the invitation.

Message Delivery

The new member does not have to recieve email messages from Minneapolis Northeast Neighbors Forum right away: you can set the delivery settings to a daily digest of topics or Web-only.

Group Message Delivery Settings (required)

The message delivery settings for the new user

 

 

 
 

Standard Profile Information

The following fields are the standard profile fields on E-Democracy.org. You must fill out all required fields; you may ignore the other fields.

Given (First) Name (required)

The name that you are commonly called, which is given to you by your parents.

Family (Last) Name (required)

The name that you inherit by birth, or acquire by marriage.

Full Name (required)

The name you want others to see. This must contain your real first and last name, but you may add a nickname.

▶ You may optionally enter other parts of the new member's profile

Biography

A description of you.
