How the membership of Minneapolis Powderhorn Neighbors Forum has changed.

There are 1198 members in the group. Normally they post around 6 posts each day; the most they have posted is 38 posts in one day. 467 members follow using the daily digest. 282 members follow only on the web.

The statistics page contains more details on the posting statistics in Minneapolis Powderhorn Neighbors Forum.

Minneapolis Powderhorn Neighbors Forum is a public group.

  • The group is visible to anyone.
  • Posts made to the group are visible to anyone.

Members of this group may post no more than 4 messages in a 24 hour period.

The Privacy and Public Life Policy details our policies on E-Democracy.org.

Please also read the full Charter and the Rules of Participation which together govern this forum. For civility and agenda-setting, we require the use of real names and no name calling. Participation will be limited for those found violating the rules.

Dave Garland is the volunteer Forum Manager and is responsible for facilitating this forum and enforcing the rules.
