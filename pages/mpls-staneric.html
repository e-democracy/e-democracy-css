Welcome to the Minneapolis Standish Ericsson Neighbors Forum

This is a place to share announcements and discuss community issues and life specific to our Standish and Ericsson neighborhoods. This forum promotes civility through the required use of real names. Join over 950 of your neighbors today.

While an independent forum, it serves as a friendly complement to our neighborhood association.

For specific forum rules, including details on what is and is not allowed in terms of commerce, see our detailed forum charter. No selling individual items, free items are fine.

You may search past messages on the this forum.

Minneapolis Standish Ericsson Neighbors Forum is a public group.

  • The group is visible to anyone.
  • Posts made to the group are visible to anyone.

The Privacy and Public Life Policy details our policies on E-Democracy.org.

Please also read the full Charter and the Rules of Participation which together govern this forum. For civility and agenda-setting, we require the use of real names and no name calling. Participation will be limited for those found violating the rules.

Steven Clift is the volunteer Forum Manager and is responsible for facilitating this forum and enforcing the rules.
