Join our new campaign to inclusively connect at least 10,000 neighbors online across St. Paul.  

Official Neighbors Forums are hosted on the trusted non-profit E-Democracy.org site, we use real names to build trust and promote useful, civil exchange. Every open forum has a local voluntee leader from your area. 

Our neighbors forums foster "community life exchange" with  local civic issues included. Our city-wide forums give you a focused place to raise public voices in local democracy.
