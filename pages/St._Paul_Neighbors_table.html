More Information

Join our new campaign to inclusively connect at least 10,000 neighbors online across St. Paul.  

Official Neighbors Forums are hosted on the trusted non-profit E-Democracy.org site, we use real names to build trust and promote useful, civil exchange. Every open forum has a local voluntee leader from your area. 

Our neighbors forums foster "community life exchange" with  local civic issues included. Our city-wide forums give you a focused place to raise public voices in local democracy. 

  • Which Neighborhood/Forum?  See Map.
  • Trouble joining? We can add you manually, e-mail your full name and desired forum to: support@e-democracy.org
  • Inclusive Community Engagement Online - Project Information
  • Contact Us to Volunteer - Or request to join our online working groups for Minneapolis, St. Paul, or organization-wide.

BeNeighbors.org is our inclusive community campaign to connect neighbors online. In the future, it will become an "open" directory of online neighborhood spaces in the Twin Cities first and then beyond.
